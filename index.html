Frequently Asked Questions

What is BullVerse India and how does it work? +

BullVerse India is an AI-powered intelligent stock exchange ecosystem. It provides real-time market data, AI-driven stock screeners, portfolio management tools, and BullMind AI assistant — all in one unified platform. You can track stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, IPOs, and crypto markets with advanced analytics and smart recommendations.

How does BullMind AI help with my investments? +

BullMind AI is your personal wealth coach that analyzes market patterns, portfolio composition, risk metrics, and sector trends. It provides personalized recommendations, portfolio diagnostics, and real-time alerts to help you make smarter investment decisions backed by data-driven insights.

What assets can I track on BullVerse? +

BullVerse supports a comprehensive range of investment assets including NSE & BSE listed stocks, mutual funds from 38+ AMCs, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), upcoming and ongoing IPOs, cryptocurrency markets, and global market indices. All trackable from a single, unified dashboard.

Is my portfolio data secure on BullVerse? +

Absolutely. BullVerse uses bank-grade encryption and secure authentication protocols including biometric verification. Your portfolio data and personal information are protected with industry-leading security measures. We never share your data with third parties.

How do I access the AI Stock Screener and Strategy Builder? +

The AI Stock Screener and Strategy Builder are accessible from the sidebar navigation. Free users get access to basic screening filters. Premium subscribers unlock advanced AI diagnostics, strategy backtesting, and customizable screening parameters with over 50+ technical and fundamental indicators.
